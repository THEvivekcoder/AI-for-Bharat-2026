## 1. Project Title
BharatSaathi: Offline AI Voice Assistant for Rural Access to Government Schemes, Jobs, and Learning

## 2. Real-World Problem

In rural India, millions of citizens fail to benefit from government schemes, skill programs, and job opportunities due to:

- Limited literacy and digital skills
- Language barriers
- Poor internet connectivity
- Complex government websites
- Lack of personalized guidance

As a result, resources exist but remain underutilized.

## 3. Goal

To build an AI-powered **offline-capable voice assistant** that enables rural citizens to:

- Discover relevant government schemes
- Find job and skill opportunities
- Access learning resources
- Receive personalized guidance in local language

Using simple voice interaction.

## 4. Why AI is Necessary

Rule-based systems cannot:

- Understand natural spoken language
- Handle multiple dialects
- Personalize suggestions
- Learn from user behaviour
- Rank relevant schemes/opportunities
- Generate simplified explanations

AI enables:

- Natural Language Understanding
- Voice interaction for low literacy users
- Intelligent recommendation engine
- Semantic search
- Personalized responses

## 5. Target Users

- Rural citizens
- Farmers
- Students in villages
- Low literacy population
- Government outreach programs

## 6. Core Capabilities

### 6.1 Voice-First Interaction
Users speak naturally in Hindi/local language and receive spoken responses.

### 6.2 Scheme Discovery Engine
AI suggests schemes based on:
- Age
- Income group
- Occupation
- Eligibility

Explains benefits in simple language.

### 6.3 Opportunity Recommender
Suggests:
- Jobs
- Skill development programs
- Scholarships
- Training

### 6.4 Learning Assistant
- Voice-based doubt solving
- Personalized learning suggestions
- Offline-friendly content

### 6.5 Low-Connectivity Mode
System works in:
- Offline mode
- Low bandwidth rural networks

## 7. Functional Requirements
